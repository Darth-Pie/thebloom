What you are walking into

A rotting town that goes quiet when you ask directions — and will not walk you up the hill.

A house at the end of the road that somehow already knows your name.

And a rare and beautiful bloom, opening for one night only, that is not a flower at all — but a countdown.

You came a long way to see something wondrous. You will. That is the problem.

Why you'll want to be there

I.

A True Mystery

Not a dungeon crawl — an investigation. Search the rooms, read what you shouldn't, follow the details that feel wrong. Every clue is real, and it all fits together. The answer is there for those brave enough to keep looking.

II.

Genuine Dread

This is horror the way it's meant to feel — you are outmatched. No power fantasy, no easy wins. Just mounting unease, a house that grows stranger the deeper you go, and a night that keeps taking things from you.

III.

Real Consequence

A single, self-contained evening with everything on the line. It's a true one-shot: choices matter, nerve matters, and not everyone walks back down the hill. How your story ends is up to you.
